Poster Projects

Sens-Plastique

Spring 2019

These three pieces are based on excerpts from Malcolm de Chazal's book of color themed aphorisms, Sens-Plastique

  • Aphorism 1: Yellow has the best "echo." Shut your eyes and it will be the last color to disappear.
  • Aphorism 2: Grey is the styx of color, the boundary line between white and black, the frontier of all colors.
  • Aphorism 3: The more colorful the landscape, the more it rests your eyes.

Two Voices Screenprint

Spring 2019

I collaborated with two students to create this piece. The print highlights and connects text and styles from Germany and Thailand. The German and Thai messages contrast each other, with the German text reading "Happiness is where you aren't" and the Thai message stating, "Happiness is here and now." The colors and structure of the poster is inspired by the grid-like Bauhaus designs, and the textures are inspired by the patterns of Thai silk fabric and textiles.
